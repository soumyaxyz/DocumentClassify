Why do we require core business functions?

CL) Why do we require core business functions?
- for a smooth day-to-day functioning of the organization and for performing tasks efficiently
and on time.
(1 List of a few common core business functions:
* Sales and Marketing
¢ — Product/Service Delivery
* Quality
* Product Development
* Accounting
* Technology
¢ Human Resource Management
¢ Supplier Management
¢ Legal and Compliance
* Corporate Planning
* Procurement (Purchases)
Q) All the above core business functions listed above might not be used in all organizations.
(1 There could be several factors for not using a core business function; for example, shortage of
resources or in a small organization, a particular process can be easily managed manually, etc.
