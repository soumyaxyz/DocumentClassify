LIST OF NON-COGNIZABLE AND BAILABLE OFFENCES UNDER INDIAN PENAL CODE
EXTRACTED FROM THE FIRST SCHEDULE TO THE CRIMINAL PROCEDURE CODE, 1973

SECTION OFFENCE

137. Deserter concealed on board merchant vessel, through negligence of master or

person in charge thereof.

154 Owner or occupier of land not giving information of riot, etc.

155 Person for whose benefit or on whose behalf a riot takes place not using all

lawful means to prevent it.

156 Agent of owner or occupier for whose benefit a riot is committed not using

all lawful means to prevent it.

166 Public servant disobeying a direction of the law with intent to cause injury

to any person.

168 Public servant unlawfully engaging in trade.

169 Public servant unlawfully buying or bidding for property.
