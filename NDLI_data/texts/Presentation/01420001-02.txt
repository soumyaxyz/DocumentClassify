Introduction

Out of the various health problems found among tribals in
India, malaria is considered as the foremost problem.

Though tribal communities constitute nearly 8% of the total
population of the country, they constitute 25% of the total
malaria cases.

Odisha is considered as highest malaria affected state in India.

Every year around 2 to 3 lakh people used to be affected from
malaria in Odisha. Around 7% of the fever cases reported from
tribal dominated district off Odisha are blamed as malaria.
