Introduction

 

e Stack is linear data structure of variable size.

¢ In array insertion and deletion can occur at any place but
in stack it can occur at only one end known a Top

¢ Insertion is known as Push and deletion is known as Pop

e Stack is known as Last In First Out (LIFO) List.

e LIFO means the last item added to the stack will be the
first item to be removed from the stack.
