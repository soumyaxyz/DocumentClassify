Introduction

 

Queue is a linear data structure.

Queue has two ends Front and Rear.

Element can be added at Rear of the queue and the
element can be removed from the Front end of the queue.
The elements of a queue are processed in the same order
as they were added into the queue.

Queues are also known as FIFO (First In Order First
Out) list or FCFS (First Come First Serve basis) list

For Example,
Queue at the ticket counter of railway station, bank, post
office, bill deposit counter etc.
