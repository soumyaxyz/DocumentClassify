Deque (Double Ended Queue)

 

Double ended queue is the linear queue data structure in which
insertion and deletion operations are not restricted to one end
but rather insertion and deletion operations can be perform on
either of the two ends. Deque data structure is also known as
Deck.

The representation of deque data structure can be shown
diagrammatically as:
