ANNEXURE

The Law Reform (Miscellaneous Provisions Amendment) Bill, 2002
A
Bill
to amend certain central enactments

BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as
follows, namely:-

Short title and commencement

1. (1) This Act may be called The Law Reform (Miscellaneous Provisions
Amendment) Act, 2002.

(2) It shall come into force on such date the Central Government may, by
notification in the Official Gazette, appoint; and different dates may be
appointed for the different provisions of this Act.

Amendment of Indian Penal Code(45 of 1860)

2. In the Indian Penal Code, after section 424, the following sections shall be inserted,
namely:-
